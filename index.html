Universal Carburetor

Universal Caburetor is an easy to install dual fuel/hybrid carburetor that enables existing petrol generators run on domestic/cooking gas(LPG), while still allowing you the option of switching to petrol whenever you run out of gas.
It comes in two sizes: Medium one for generators of 2kva-3.6kva and big one for generators of 3.8kva-11kva capacity. Our carburetors are not compatible with Yamaha and the smallest Tiger Generators(I beta pass my neighbour)
